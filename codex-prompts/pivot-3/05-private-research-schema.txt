Design the private research schema for Under the Radar-style stock recommendations.

Goal:
- Create structured models that make subscribed research easier to understand without
  redistributing full source content.

Models should include:
- PrivateResearchDocument
- StockRecommendation
- RecommendationChange
- ThesisPoint
- RiskPoint
- ValuationNote
- Catalyst
- PortfolioWatchItem
- SourceExcerpt
- PersonalActionQuestion

Fields should capture:
- company name
- ticker
- exchange
- recommendation/source rating
- stated target price or valuation if present
- recommendation date
- time horizon
- thesis
- bullish arguments
- bearish arguments
- risks
- catalysts
- valuation assumptions
- numbers to verify
- source citation
- confidence score

Requirements:
- Keep excerpts short.
- Store source document references and page/section when available.
- Include validation and example JSON.
- Add tests.

Acceptance criteria:
- Schema supports summarizing stock recommendations without copying full articles.
- Validation rejects malformed ratings/confidence values.
