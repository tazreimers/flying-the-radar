Pivot 3: private personal-use Under the Radar research companion.

Define the product boundaries and architecture for personal-use ingestion of subscribed
Under the Radar material.

Goal:
- Help a subscriber privately ingest, organize, summarize, and understand Under the Radar
  research they are already entitled to access.
- Do not redistribute paid content.
- Do not provide personalized financial advice.

Important constraints:
- A login does not automatically grant the right to scrape, copy, store, or redistribute
  paywalled content.
- Prefer user-provided PDFs/emails/downloads over automated login scraping.
- Any logged-in automation must be disabled by default and require explicit user confirmation
  that their subscription terms permit it.
- This branch is for single-user/private use first.

Requirements:
- Add a private-research architecture separate from the public daily brief.
- Create docs explaining:
  - personal-use scope
  - no redistribution
  - copyright and subscription terms caution
  - no financial advice
  - source attribution
- Design modules for:
  - private ingestion
  - secure credential/secrets handling
  - document library
  - recommendation extraction
  - personal digest
  - search/Q&A
  - password-protected local UI
- Preserve existing PDF summarization functionality.

Acceptance criteria:
- Clear docs and module boundaries exist.
- No logged-in scraper is implemented yet.
- Tests remain offline.
